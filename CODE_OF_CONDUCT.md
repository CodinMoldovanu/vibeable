# Code of Conduct

Vibeable contributors are expected to keep participation professional, respectful, and focused on the work. Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not tolerated.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact configured on the future public repository. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
